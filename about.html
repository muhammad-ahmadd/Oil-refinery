The Company

Our Mission | Endure industry with friendly, reliable, and cost-effective engineering support.

History And Background

Founded as a small engineering company. Today, the company is not only one of largest in MENA & South Asia but also one of the most respected engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) companies, executing some of the biggest and most challenging projects in the region. We are backed by UKS, a top-ranking global construction company, family enterprise with longstanding ties to the local business community.

Nearing our 40th year in operations, our achievements provide inspiration for the future. Through our continued growth and expansion, we remain focused on new technological innovations and incremental facility improvements that reduce waste or risk to people and the environment.

DoubleE's Excellence

DoubleE has guiding principles that profoundly influence our interactions, client relationships, and pursuit of excellence. These principles define our approach to respect, integrity, collaboration, and continuous improvement. By upholding these values, we foster a culture that prioritizes empathy, professionalism, and delivering exceptional results.

Principles

Numerous guiding principles shape how we treat one another, our clients, and how we achieve excellence. These principles serve as the foundation of our organization and are deeply ingrained in our culture. We believe in upholding these principles in every interaction, decision, and endeavor we undertake.

Respect

We value and respect every individual, embracing diversity and treating everyone with dignity. We foster an inclusive environment where ideas are heard, contributions are acknowledged, and differences are celebrated.

Integrity

We conduct ourselves with the highest level of integrity and honesty. We are committed to ethical practices, transparency, and accountability in all our actions. Trust is paramount in building strong relationships, and we strive to uphold it at all times.

Customer Focus

Our clients are at the center of everything we do. We are committed to understanding their needs, exceeding their expectations, and building long-lasting relationships based on trust and mutual success. We listen attentively, communicate effectively, and deliver solutions that address their unique challenges.

Professionalism

We maintain a professional and ethical approach in all our interactions. We adhere to industry best practices, follow established guidelines and policies, and demonstrate professionalism in our demeanor, communication, and appearance. We take pride in representing our organization with professionalism and integrity.

Adaptability

In a constantly evolving business landscape, we embrace change and adapt quickly to new challenges and opportunities. We are agile and flexible, open to new ideas and perspectives, and willing to embrace innovative solutions to meet the evolving needs of our clients and the industry.

Mission
