HEAT EXCHANGERS RETUBING AND MANUFACTURING

Retubing: DoubleE Heat exchanger retubing services involve removing the damaged or fouled tubes from the heat exchanger and replacing them with new tubes under the TEMA standards. With specialized equipment and expertise, the process documentation and procedures are prepared for each type of exchanger. Our retubing process typically includes the following steps: - The heat exchanger is inspected to identify the extent of the damage or fouling. - The damaged tubes are removed from the heat exchanger using specialized equipment. - The interior of the heat exchanger's tubes is thoroughly cleaned to remove any debris or fouling. - New tubes are carefully installed, welded, and rolled at torque setting to ensure proper alignment. - The heat exchanger is tested to ensure that it is functioning properly and efficiently. Our Heat exchanger retubing services guarantee efficiency, reliability, and extend the lifespan of the equipment. Manufacturing: DoubleE has in-house specialized capabilities, knowledge, equipment, and expertise for designing and manufacturing all types of Heat Exchangers as per TEMA and ASME Standards. DoubleE also works with reputable and experienced heat exchanger manufacturers worldwide to ensure that the heat exchanger is fabricated and tested to meet the specific requirements of the clients.
